About Us

Good News Transportation LLC is dedicated to providing safe, reliable, and compassionate transportation for children with autism, from elementary to high school. We understand the unique needs of each child and prioritize comfort, safety, and a structured routine to help families with special transportation needs. Our experienced and trained drivers ensure a stress-free ride, making every journey smooth and enjoyable for both parents and children.
